Personal Project: WeatherTmr

WeatherTmr


Built a service which, given your phone number and zipcode, checks tomorrow's weather in your area daily. Should the weather require any special preparation (ie rain, snow, extremely hot) it will text you the day before it happens, letting you know "Look's like it'll rain tomorrow - better bring a raincoat!". Built using HTML/CSS, PHP, MySQL, and Javascript. Used API's from Twilio and Wunderground.
